Testing mixins

The default color of each paragraph is light grey. Breakpoint Slicer is used to change the color to black at certain window widths.

+at(3)
  background-color: black

+from(3)
  background-color: black

+to(3)
  background-color: black

+between(2, 4)
  background-color: black
